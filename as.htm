About the movie Aravinda Sametha Veera Raghava

Aravinda Sametha Veera Raghava is a Telugu action movie about an angry young man seeking to avenge his father’s killing, but his perception of life changes after he meets a woman. The chance meeting with the woman makes him realise that violence begets only violence. She becomes his guide on the journey of non-violence and self-realisation.

Release Date of Aravinda Sametha Veera Raghava movie on OTT

The Telugu movie was released across cinema halls on 11th October, 2018. It is also streaming on the ZEE5 OTT platform.

Plot of Aravinda Sametha Veera Raghava movie 

Basi Reddy and Narappa Reddy are the leaders of two villages and have been bitter rivals for decades. Veera Raghava is Narappa’s son who is just back from London after completing his studies. On the way home, Basi’s men attack and kill Narapa, and an enraged Veera Raghava draws a sword and slaughters his foes. 

Fed up with the violence, Veera Raghava goes to Hyderabad where he falls in love with Aravinda, who guides him on a quest of self-realisation and non-violence. But is Veera Raghava successful in winning over Basi Reddy? Watch this  Telugu action-drama movie to find out.

Aravinda Sametha Veera Raghava movie cast and crew

This Telugu drama film has cast N.T. Rama Rao Jr. as Veera Raghava Reddy, Pooja Hegde as Aravindha, Jagapathi Babu as Basi Reddy, Nagendra Babu as Narappa Reddy, Naveen Chandra as Bala Reddy, Supriya Pathak as Jeji, Eesha Rebba as Sunandha, and V.K. Naresh as Saradhi.

Aravinda Sametha Veera Raghava is written and directed by Trivikram Srinivas and produced by S. Radha Krishna under the label Haarika & Hassine Creations. The cinematography of the movie has been handled by P.S. Vinod.

Interesting facts about Aravinda Sametha Veera Raghava

1. The shirtless battle scene featuring N.T. Rama Rao Jr. was filmed in a dry, open landscape under temperatures around 43 C.

2. This was the first joint venture film between Jr. NTR and Trivikram Srinivas.

3. This action movie’s poster was made publicly available on the occasion of Jr. NTR’s birthday, on 20th May, 1983.
